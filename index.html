Gundam Timelines

The Gundam franchise spans multiple alternate timelines, each with its own unique story and setting.

U.C.

Universal Century

The original and most expansive timeline. From the One Year War to Hathaway's Flash, humanity's conflict between Earth and the space colonies continues across generations.

C.E.

Cosmic Era

A world divided between genetically enhanced Coordinators and natural-born humans. The SEED series explores the cycle of war and the pursuit of peace.

P.D.

Post Disaster

300 years after the Calamity War, child soldiers of Tekkadan fight for their place in a corrupt world. Iron-Blooded Orphans redefines what it means to survive.

A.D.

Anno Domini

Set in our own calendar, Celestial Being deploys Gundams to end global conflict. Gundam 00 explores intervention, idealism, and mutual understanding.
